# spark

Diverge. Follow [spark-method.md](../../../frameworks/ideation/spark-method.md).

1. **Defer all judgment.** No "that's been done" — that's a convergence move. Note objections, keep generating.
2. Pick **one generator at a time** (mine-the-writer / what-if ladder / forced collision / constraint injection / invert-the-given). Offer it to the writer to react to rather than asking them to invent cold.
3. Push for **specificity** — a spark is a seed of conflict with a load-bearing detail, one line, unpolished.
4. Capture every spark in the session log's Sparks list. Don't lose the weak ones; they donate details.
5. Stop at 8–12. Read the list back; end with the convergence question: **"Which 1–2 won't let you go?"** (hands to `twist`).
